# Hembree Bell Law Firm - Evidence Repository

## Overview

This repository documents evidence related to the legal representation provided by Hembree Bell law firm in a divorce case between Jennifer Lyssy and Marty Hale.

## Case Background

- **Client**: Jennifer Lyssy
- **Case Type**: Divorce proceedings (Lyssy vs. Hale)
- **Law Firm**: Hembree Bell
- **Total Fees Paid**: $104,000

## Issues with Representation

This repository serves as a collection point for evidence demonstrating:

1. **Poor Legal Representation**: Inadequate legal services despite significant fees paid
2. **Lack of Communication**: Consistent failure to communicate with the client
3. **Adverse Outcomes**: Complete loss of assets and business interests

## Case Outcome

Despite paying $104,000 in legal fees, Jennifer Lyssy experienced the following outcomes:
- Loss of business interests
- Loss of property
- Loss of all marital assets
- No favorable settlement or division of assets
